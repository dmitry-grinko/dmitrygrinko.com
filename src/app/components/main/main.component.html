My background

I graduated from Brest State Technical University (Belarus) with a degree in Civil Engineering (2005–2010).

After graduation, I worked as a design engineer in architectural and construction companies in Belarus, mainly on residential projects.

In 2015 I started learning programming. I began with web and mobile development, and later moved into backend engineering and cloud systems.

In 2018, my family and I immigrated to the United States.

Today I work as an Application Developer at Portland General Electric, building backend services and integrations in AWS.
